Why was
Stratum XR created?

Stratum XR was born from the need to move beyond 2D slices and traditional top-down views. We wanted to provide a truly immersive way to understand subsurface structures, making data interpretation more intuitive and collaborative.

Traditional tools were limited by hardware and perspective. By leveraging cloud computing and VR technology, we enable users to walk through their datasets in real-world context, revealing patterns that were previously hidden in complex GPR signals.
